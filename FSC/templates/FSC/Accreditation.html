Key Standards and Expectations

1.6: Scholarship and Academic Support

Chapters must designate a scholarship/academic chairperson to manage academic initiatives and provide resources for members' success. Submit Academic Chairperson Information.

2.5: Chapter Advisor Requirements

Chapters must have an active Chapter Advisor who provides guidance, supports recruitment, and completes biannual reports. Submit Advisor Accreditation Information | Submit Advisor ID Credentials.

2.7 & 2.8: Social Media and Website Maintenance

Chapters should maintain an active social media presence and keep their websites updated for improved visibility. Submit Social Media Details.

5.1: Community Service

Each chapter must complete at least five community service hours per member per semester. Submit Community Service Records.
