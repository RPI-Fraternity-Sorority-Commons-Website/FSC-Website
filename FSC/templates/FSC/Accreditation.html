Part III: Standards

1.6: Scholarship/Academic Chairperson

The chapter has a scholarship/academic chairperson who maintains and manages the chapter's scholastic efforts. Complete the Academic Chairperson Form to provide details about your chapter's academic initiatives.

2.5: Chapter Advisor Requirements

Chapters will have a listed primary Chapter Advisor who:

  • Is actively involved with the chapter's activities.
  • Participates in recruitment events and attends meetings regularly.
  • Completes biannual Advisor Reports.
  • Attends at least one in-person FSC Advisor Meeting.
Advisor Accreditation Form | Advisor ID Credentials Form

2.7 & 2.8: Social Media & Website Presence

Chapters are required to maintain a social media presence and are encouraged to develop a website for public relations. Complete the Social Media Footprint Form.

5.1: Community Service

Chapters must complete an average of at least 5 hours of community service per member per semester. Community Service Form.

Philanthropic Efforts

Though not explicitly part of this year’s accreditation, chapters are required to record and report all philanthropic activities. Philanthropic Efforts Form.

By submitting these forms, your chapter will help us track the collective efforts of the FSC Community and fulfill our shared goals for the year.
