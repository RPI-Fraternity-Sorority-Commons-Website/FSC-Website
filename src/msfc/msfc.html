Multicultural Sorority and Fraternity Council (MSFC)

Multicultural Sorority and Fraternity Commons

The Multicutural Sorority and Fraternity Commons (MSFC) is home to four intercutural organizations, these organizations represent both fraternities and soroities and boasts organizations that are affiliated with National APIDA Panehellenic Association (NAPA), National Association of Latino Fraternal Organizations, Inc. (NALFO), and the National Pan-Hellenic Council (NPHC).

Member Organizations:

  • Fraternities
    • Alpha Phi Alpha - ΑΦΑ
    • Phi Iota Alpha - ΦΙΑ
    • Pi Delta Psi - ΠΔΨ
  • Sororities
    • Omega Phi Beta - ΩΦΒ
