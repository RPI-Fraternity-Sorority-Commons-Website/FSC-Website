About Us

Welcome to the dynamic and active world of Greek life at Rensselaer! This site is meant to give you the opportunity to learn more about our different organizations on campus, so you can begin to consider if Greek life is right for you or your student.

At the center of our community is the Fraternity and Soroity Commons. Our council plays a vital role in shaping your Greek life experience. Tasked with planning community events, programs, and representing our community as a whole, they ensure a positive and enriching experience for all members.

Our Councils:

Our social organizations fall under one of three councils, including the Interfraternity Council (IFC), Multicultural Soroity and Fraternity Council, and Panhellenic Counil (Panhel). In addition to these we also have several professional organizations on campus.

Eligibility:

RPI uses a deferred recruitment system, meaning students are not elgible to join a social organization until the seccond semester of their freshman year. For transfer students, they must have transferred in with at least 12 credit hours to join. Regardless of when a student joins, they must meet the following criteria before they can partake in recruitment:

  • Potential new members must have a minimum of 12 credit hours
  • They must have a cummulative average of at least 2.5, or meet their respective organization's criteria, whichever is higher
  • Transfer students must have had a 2.5 minimum GPA the semester before, at their previous institution
  • First-year January admits are not elgible until the following Fall
  • They must be in good standing with the University
    Note: Exceptions are made for our professional gender-inclusive Professional Fraternity/Soroity
