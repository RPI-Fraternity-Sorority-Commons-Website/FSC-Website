Scholarships

Fraternities and sororities within the Fraternity & Sorority Commons offer more than just social engagement—they are communities focused on:

  • Academic success
  • Leadership development
  • Personal growth

As part of our commitment to supporting student achievement, several of our organizations provide scholarship opportunities for both incoming students and current members.

Each scholarship has its own set of requirements and deadlines, so applicants should carefully review the details before applying.

Sigma Phi Epsilon – Balanced Man Scholarship

This scholarship is open to first-year RPI students who demonstrate:

  • Academic excellence
  • Leadership skills
  • A commitment to well-being

Application Close Date: November 1, 2024

President Contact: president@sigepnyd.com

Alpha Sigma Phi – To Better the Man Scholarship

This scholarship is available to all full-time undergraduate men who exemplify the fraternity’s values:

  • Silence
  • Charity
  • Purity
  • Honor
  • Patriotism

Application Close Date: October 1, 2024

President Contact: asigphi.president@gmail.com

Acacia – Men of Action Scholarship

This scholarship offers four $500 awards to undergraduate RPI students this semester.

Application Close Date: October 26, 2024

President Contact: rpi.akak.pres@gmail.com

For more information on these and other opportunities, visit the scholarship links below. Don’t miss your chance to access free money for your education!

IFC Scholarship Opportunities

Scholarship Opportunities

Every year, Sigma Phi Epsilon awards their Balanced Man Scholarship to RPI students who exhibit academic excellence, demonstrate leadership skills, and display commitment to pursuing holistic health and well-being. All first-year RPI students are eligible for the scholarship. You will be asked to provide personal, academic, and extracurricular information during the application.

At Alpha Sigma Phi, our mission is to Better the World through Better Men, and through this scholarship we honor and reward men who exemplify our values — Silence, Charity, Purity, Honor, and Patriotism. All undergraduate men enrolled as full-time students this academic term are eligible to receive this scholarship. Applicants can expect to schedule an interview within two weeks of submission.

This semester Acacia is providing 4 undergraduate RPI students a chance to receive $500 scholarships! Any undergraduate student at RPI is eligible.
