Scholarships

Fraternities and sororities within the Fraternity & Sorority Commons offer more than just social engagement—they are communities focused on:

  • Academic success
  • Leadership development
  • Personal growth

As part of our commitment to supporting student achievement, several of our organizations provide scholarship opportunities for both incoming students and current members.

Each scholarship has its own set of requirements and deadlines, so applicants should carefully review the details before applying.

Sigma Phi Epsilon – Balanced Man Scholarship

This scholarship is open to first-year RPI students who demonstrate:

  • Academic excellence
  • Leadership skills
  • A commitment to well-being

Application Close Date: November 1, 2024

President Contact: president@sigepnyd.com

Alpha Sigma Phi – To Better the Man Scholarship

This scholarship is available to all full-time undergraduate men who exemplify the fraternity’s values:

  • Silence
  • Charity
  • Purity
  • Honor
  • Patriotism

Application Close Date: October 1, 2024

President Contact: asigphi.president@gmail.com

Acacia – Men of Action Scholarship

This scholarship offers four $500 awards to undergraduate RPI students this semester.

Application Close Date: October 26, 2024

President Contact: rpi.akak.pres@gmail.com
